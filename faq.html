Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is RakshakAI?

A: An AI-powered platform to protect and support abuse victims with legal and emotional help.

Q2: Is RakshakAI free to use?

A: Yes. Our core services are free and accessible to everyone in need.

Q3: How is my data protected?

A: We use secure, encrypted storage and never share your data without consent.

Q4: Can I get emergency help through RakshakAI?

A: Yes. You can trigger a panic alert to notify your trusted contacts or NGOs instantly.

Q5: Does RakshakAI provide legal advice?

A: We offer AI-guided legal information and help connect you to real human lawyers.

Q6: Can men also use RakshakAI?

A: Absolutely. RakshakAI is gender-neutral and built to help all abuse victims equally.

Q7: Will RakshakAI work in rural areas?

A: Yes, even with low network. It’s lightweight and works with basic smartphones too.

Q8: How can I support RakshakAI?

A: You can donate, volunteer, or share our platform with someone who might need help.
