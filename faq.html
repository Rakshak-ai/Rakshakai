Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is RakshakAI?

RakshakAI is an AI-powered app that provides legal and emotional support to victims of abuse or injustice. It's your personal safety assistant, available 24x7.

Q2. Is RakshakAI free to use?

Yes. The basic features like SOS alerts, legal information, and emotional support are completely free for all users in need.

Q3. How does the SOS system work?

When you trigger SOS, your location and alert message are instantly shared with selected contacts or NGOs. Emergency services may also be notified (if enabled).

Q4. Can I remain anonymous while using the app?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. You can use RakshakAI anonymously without revealing personal details.

Q5. Is my data safe?

Yes. We follow strict encryption and privacy protocols. Your data is never shared without your consent and is stored securely.

Q6. Does RakshakAI work offline?

Some features like local legal guides and safety tips work offline. For real-time alerts, an internet connection is recommended.

Q7. Who can benefit from RakshakAI?

Anyone facing domestic violence, harassment, injustice, or in need of urgent help. It’s built for everyone — regardless of gender, age, or background.

Q8. How can I support RakshakAI’s mission?

You can donate, spread awareness, or partner with us. Every contribution helps protect more lives. Visit our Donate page for details.
