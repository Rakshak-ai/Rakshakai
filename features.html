Key Features of RakshakAI

  • Real-Time SOS Alerts: One tap sends your location and distress message to your trusted contacts and NGOs.
  • AI-Powered Legal Guidance: Chatbot explains legal rights and suggests relevant IPC sections based on user input.
  • Evidence Locker: Encrypted cloud-based locker to safely store video, audio, images, and documents as evidence.
  • Voice & Gesture Activation: Activate alerts silently with a secret voice command or hand gesture.
  • Emotional Support Chatbot: Friendly AI that listens, offers emotional support, and suggests mental wellness tips.
  • Multilingual Support: Available in Hindi, English, and regional languages for maximum accessibility.
  • Offline Functionality: Emergency features work without internet using SMS fallback mode.
