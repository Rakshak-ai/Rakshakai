Our Vision

RakshakAI ka lakshya hai har us vyakti tak suraksha pahunchana jo kisi bhi roop me mental, emotional, ya physical abuse se guzar raha ho. Hum AI ke madhyam se legal support, emotional counseling, aur real-time help ek hi platform par dete hain. Har shikayat, har aawaz, har aansu important hai.

Key Features

Abuse ke khilaaf AI-powered protection

Real-Time SOS

Ek tap par turant alert & location send to nearby volunteers & police.

AI Legal Chatbot

Hindi & English me kanooni salah 24/7 bina kisi human delay ke.

Emotional Support

Anonymous chat with empathetic AI trained in trauma response.

Evidence Vault

Audio, video, aur messages securely store karein future legal action ke liye.

Volunteer Network

Trusted NGOs aur helpers ka verified network aapke aas paas.

Offline Access

Bina internet ke bhi SOS aur voice logging feature kaam karta hai.
