Because Every Victim Deserves a Protector

RakshakAI is India’s first AI-powered system built to protect victims of abuse, injustice, and violence in real-time. Emotion + Law + AI — in one powerful app.

🔍 About RakshakAI

RakshakAI is a mission-driven platform that offers real-time support, legal guidance, and emergency alerts for victims of abuse. With emotional support modules and AI-based recognition, it is your digital rakshak when the world turns away.

🛡️ Core Features

📢 SOS Alert

Real-time emergency alerts to trusted contacts or authorities.

⚖️ Legal Aid Chatbot

Instant legal help based on IPC laws and real case guidance.

🧠 Emotional Support

In-built mental health tools for stress and trauma recovery.
