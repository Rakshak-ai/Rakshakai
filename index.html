Frequently Asked Questions

Janiye RakshakAI ke baare mein sabse zyada pooche gaye sawalon ke jawab.

RakshakAI kya hai aur yeh kis liye bana hai?

RakshakAI ek AI-powered mobile app hai jo abuse victims ko legal aur emotional support deta hai — real-time alert system ke saath.

Kya yeh app free hai ya paise lagenge?

App ka core version bilkul free hai. Kuch premium features NGO ya legal experts ke liye available honge in future.

Meri personal information safe rahegi?

Bilkul. RakshakAI users ka data fully encrypted format mein store karta hai. Aapki privacy hamare liye sabse pehle hai.

Main donate kaise kar sakta hoon?

Aap donate.html page par jaakar UPI QR ya Razorpay gateway se direct contribution kar sakte hain.

Yeh app kis team ne banaya hai?

RakshakAI ka nirmaan Manvender Singh (Founder) ne kiya hai, jinka mission hai: "Because Every Victim Deserves a Protector."

NGO ya lawyers isse kaise use kar sakte hain?

NGO ke liye special onboarding version aayega jisme verified cases, legal tools aur tracking integrated honge.
